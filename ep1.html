What is Africa?

Africa is a continent.
It is one of the 7 big land areas in the world.

Where is Africa?

It is below Europe and next to Asia.
It is surrounded by oceans — the Atlantic Ocean and the Indian Ocean.

What is in Africa?

54 countries, like Egypt, Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, and Morocco.

Many people — over 1.4 billion!

Many animals — lions, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and more.

Many languages — people speak over 2,000 different languages.

Different land — deserts (like the Sahara), forests, rivers (like the Nile), and mountains.

Geography

Africa has a wide range of landscapes:

Sahara Desert – the largest hot desert in the world.

Mount Kilimanjaro – the highest mountain in Africa.

Nile River – the longest river in the world.

Savannas, rainforests, lakes, and coastlines.
